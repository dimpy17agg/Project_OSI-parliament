Model Youth Parliament (MY Parliament)


Model Youth Parliament organizes various events time to time towards achieving its mission. we gives a platform for young mind to discuss about legislation and also develop an interest towards policies.Every year three simulations of parliamentary sessions are organized in line with the Parliamentary seesions,namely budget,monsoon and winter season. People across the country from different educational background participate in this event to discuss current issue of national importance.
